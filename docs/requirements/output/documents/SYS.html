Test document for development of Doorstop

1.1 SYS001

The project shall progress through 4 validated stages: SITL, RL Training, HIL, and Custom Hardware, with formal gate reviews between each.

Child links: FW001, HW001, RL001, SAF001, SIM001

1.2 SYS002

The end-to-end test suite shall include SITL regression, HIL regression, and custom hardware integration test campaigns with documented results.

1.3 SYS003

A quantitative performance comparison (PID baseline vs. RL) shall be generated across all stages (SITL, HIL, hardware).

1.4 SYS004

All technical documentation, user manuals, operating procedures, and design rationale shall be completed and archived in version control before project closure.

1.5 SYS005

The total system shall complete integration of 132 work packages across 6 phases within an estimated 42-53 weeks and ~1,304 person-hours.

1.6 SYS006

Each stage gate shall produce a documented go/no-go decision with exit criteria checklist, entry criteria checklist, and formal authorization.

1.7 SYS007

The system shall use ROS 2 Jazzy as the middleware for inter-process communication in SITL and HIL configurations.

1.8 SYS008

CPU load shall be monitored in flight; load >80% shall generate a WARNING and >95% shall generate a CRITICAL alert.

1.9 SYS009

RAM usage shall be monitored in flight; usage >90% shall generate a WARNING and >98% shall generate a CRITICAL alert.
