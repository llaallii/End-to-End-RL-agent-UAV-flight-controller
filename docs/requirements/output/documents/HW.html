Test document for development of Doorstop

1.1 HW001

The custom flight controller PCB shall provide regulated 5 V and 3.3 V power rails with voltage ripple <50 mV.

Parent links: SYS001

1.2 HW002

The PCB shall use a >=4-layer stackup with dedicated power and ground planes.

1.3 HW003

The PCB shall provide I2C and SPI interfaces for sensor connectivity, UART/USB for communication, and >=4 PWM outputs for motor ESCs.

1.4 HW004

The hardware requirements shall define operating temperature range, vibration, and moisture tolerance appropriate for UAV flight.

1.5 HW005

The PCB design shall pass all Design Rule Checks (DRC) with zero violations prior to fabrication release.

1.6 HW006

All power rails shall measure within specification and the MCU shall boot and communicate before the board is accepted.

1.7 HW007

The Bill of Materials shall identify all components with manufacturer part numbers, and all long-lead items shall have backup alternates sourced.
