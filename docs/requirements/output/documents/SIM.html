Test document for development of Doorstop

1.1 SIM001

The SITL simulation environment shall execute at >=1x real-time speed on the development workstation.

Parent links: SYS001

1.1 SIM010

The simulated IMU shall inject white noise with statistics matching MEMS IMU specifications (e.g., MPU-6050).

1.2 SIM002

The Gazebo simulator shall render the UAV environment at >=30 FPS with GUI enabled.

1.2 SIM020

The SITL test campaign shall achieve >=90% requirement coverage as documented in the verification matrix.

1.3 SIM003

The physics simulation shall use a timestep of <=0.004 s (>=250 Hz).

1.4 SIM004

The simulation shall sustain stable physics execution for >=10 minutes without drift or crash.

1.5 SIM005

The UAV dynamics model shall implement full 6-DOF rigid-body equations of motion (3 translational + 3 rotational).

1.6 SIM006

The motor model shall simulate first-order thrust response with a time constant of 50-100 ms.

1.7 SIM007

The UAV model shall have a total mass of approximately 1 kg with documented inertia tensor.

1.8 SIM008

The aerodynamic model shall produce drag forces that oppose vehicle motion and produce a realistic terminal velocity.

1.9 SIM009

The IMU sensor model shall publish accelerometer and gyroscope data at >=100 Hz.

1.11 SIM011

The sensor coordinate frames shall be defined in the URDF/SDF model and published as a correct TF tree.

1.12 SIM012

The simulation world shall include at least 2 distinct world files (e.g., indoor flat, outdoor with obstacles).

1.13 SIM013

The PID baseline controller shall achieve stable hover for >=60 seconds in simulation.

1.14 SIM014

The PID controller shall maintain position accuracy within +/-0.5 m during hover.

1.15 SIM015

The PID controller shall maintain attitude accuracy within +/-5 degrees during hover.

1.16 SIM016

The PID altitude controller shall hold altitude within +/-0.3 m and settle within 3-5 s of a setpoint change.

1.17 SIM017

The PID controller shall exhibit overshoot of <30% in step response for all controlled axes.

1.18 SIM018

The attitude control loop shall execute at >=100 Hz.

1.19 SIM019

The SITL verification suite shall include >=10 defined test scenarios that are automated and repeatable.

1.21 SIM021

Disturbance rejection tests shall demonstrate controller recovery without instability following applied perturbations.
